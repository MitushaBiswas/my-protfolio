Recent Projects

PARASPEAK

Sign Language to Speech Converter

PARASPEAK is an assistive device that uses IoT and Machine Learning to translate sign language into spoken words. Designed to help the deaf and hard-of-hearing, it captures hand gestures using sensors and a microcontroller, processes them in real-time, and outputs speech via a connected mobile app.

Fire Fighting Robot

Obstacle-Avoiding Safety Bot

A smart robot developed using Arduino UNO and ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles and navigate around them. The robot is designed to detect fire and avoid collisions while moving autonomously, making it suitable for fire-prone industrial environments.

Photocatalytic Tiles

Pollution-Reducing Eco Tiles

These innovative concrete tiles use photocatalytic reactions activated by sunlight to reduce air pollution. An energy-free, low-maintenance solution for public pathways, these tiles promote sustainability by purifying the air as people walk over them.
